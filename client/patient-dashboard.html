Hospital Guidelines

  • Dinacharya (Daily Routine): Wake up before sunrise (Brahma Muhurta) to synchronize with nature's rhythms.
  • Dietary Rules: Consume only warm, freshly prepared Ayurvedic meals provided by the kitchen. Avoid outside food strictly.
  • Hydration: Drink warm water or prescribed herbal teas throughout the day. Avoid cold water.
  • Rest: Ensure adequate daytime rest post-treatments. Avoid sleeping immediately after meals.
  • Technology Detox: Minimize screen time, especially during treatments and before bedtime to promote mental calmness.
  • Hygiene: Maintain personal hygiene. Wear loose, comfortable cotton clothing.

Diet Practices

⚠️ Avoid / Reduce

  • White flour food: Reduce white bread, pizza, pasta, momos, pancake, noodles etc.
  • Hot & Spicy food: Limit hot & spicy food like red & green chilly.
  • Sour & Acidic food: Reduce vinegar, pickles, lemon.
  • Yogurt, Lassi & Curd: Avoid at night. Do not consume during cough & cold, muscle stiffness, or tension.
  • Heavy & Deep-fried food: Avoid deep-fried, oily, and heavy foods. Limit meat, fish, and chicken (prefer in soup form).
  • Processed & Cold items: Avoid processed, preserved, and frozen foods, cold drinks, carbonated drinks, ice-cold water, and cold showers.
  • Stimulants: Avoid alcohol, smoking, and black tea or coffee on an empty stomach.
  • Raw vegetables: Avoid raw vegetables (onions, broccoli, cabbage, beetroot, carrot, lettuce). Cooked, steamed, or boiled vegetables are better.
  • Lentils & Legumes: Limit black lentils. For other beans/lentils (Urad dal, Arhar dal, Chana dal, Rajma, legumes), soak in water overnight before cooking the next day.
  • Refined sugar: Avoid white sugar products, refined sugar, chocolate, candy, pastry, cakes etc.

✨ Good / Recommended

  • Water: Drink more water, but not immediately after meals (wait for one hour).
  • Whole Grains: Prefer brown flour, grains & cereals like amaranth, oats, whole wheat, rice, quinoa, barley etc.
  • Vegetables: Eat boiled, cooked & steamed vegetables, green leafy vegetables, soups, asparagus, green beans, cooked garlic & onion, carrots etc.
  • Fruits & Sprouts: Enjoy papaya, banana, pomegranates, berries, cherries, grapes, peaches, mango or other seasonal fruits. Sprouted beans, moong bean sprouts, alfalfa sprouts etc. (If prone to gas or bloating, consume in cooked form).
  • Spices & Herbs: Use turmeric, ginger, Himalayan salt, black pepper, coriander, cloves, cumin seeds, fenugreek seed, thyme seed, ajwain, cinnamon, cardamom, asafetida etc.
  • Beverages: Enjoy herbal tea, ginger tulsi tea, mint tea, and aloe vera juice.
  • Dairy: Consume cow or buffalo milk cooked with turmeric & black pepper or cooked with cardamom, fennel or saffron (curcuma), cow ghee, goat milk etc.
  • Healthy Oils: Use cow ghee, mustard oil, coconut oil, olive oil, or sunflower oil for cooking.
  • Soaked Dried Fruits: Soak raisins, dates, figs, and prunes in water overnight to eat the next day.
  • Soaked Nuts: Soak dry fruits and nuts (like almonds) in water overnight to eat the next day.
  • Natural Sweets: Limit to honey, fruit juice, jaggery, brown sugar etc.
